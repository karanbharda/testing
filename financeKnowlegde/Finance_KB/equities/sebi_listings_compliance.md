# SEBI Listing Rules & Compliance Framework

## SEBI (Listing Obligations & Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015

### Listing Process

**Pre-Listing Requirements:**
1. **Eligibility Criteria**
   - Minimum net worth: ₹25 crore
   - Minimum IPO size: ₹10 crore
   - Promoter contribution: Minimum 20%
   - Track record: 3 years of profitable operation

2. **Prospectus Filing**
   - SEBI approval required
   - Disclosure of all material facts
   - Risk factors documentation
   - Auditor certification of financials

3. **IPO Process**
   - Book building: Price discovery
   - Offer period: Minimum 3 days
   - Allotment: Random basis
   - Refund/Credit: T+3 basis

### Continuous Disclosure Obligations

**Financial Statements:**
- Quarterly Results: Within 45 days of quarter end
- Annual Reports: Within 60 days of financial year end
- Unaudited financials: Within 30 days of quarter end
- Audit reports: Complete with all schedules

**Material Events:**
- Acquisition/Divestment: >10% of net assets
- Directors appointment/resignation
- Change in auditors
- Major contracts/tenders
- Insolvency proceedings
- Dividend declarations

**Disclosure Timeline:**
- Immediate: Within 30 minutes of event occurrence
- Within 24 hours: Official announcement
- Within 3 days: Details submission to SEBI

### Board of Directors Requirements

**Board Composition:**
- Independent directors: Minimum 1/3 of board
- Audit Committee: All independent directors
- Nomination & Remuneration Committee
- Stakeholders Relationship Committee
- Risk Management Committee (for large cap)

**Director Qualification:**
- Age limit: Usually 75 years
- Not disqualified under Companies Act
- Expertise in relevant fields
- Declaration of directorships in other companies

### Related Party Transactions

**RPT Policy:**
- Board approved policy required
- Audit committee approval
- Public disclosure
- Arm's length price validation

**Exemptions:**
- Transactions < ₹1 crore
- Salary & remuneration to directors
- Transactions in ordinary course

## Stock Exchange Requirements

### NSE/BSE Listing Rules

**Listing Fees:**
- Based on IPO size
- Annual maintenance fees
- Segment-wise listing
- Multiple segment listing optional

**Equity Share Requirements:**
- Par value typically ₹1, ₹2, ₹5, or ₹10
- Denomination consistency
- Tradability classification

### Delisting Process

**Voluntary Delisting:**
- Board approval
- Shareholder approval (90% of votes)
- Regulatory approval
- Exit price determination
- 6-month trading window

**Compulsory Delisting:**
- Non-compliance with SEBI rules
- Insolvency proceedings
- Negative net worth
- Failure to meet minimum shareholding

## Takeover Code & M&A Requirements

**Takeover Code (SEBI) 1997:**
1. **Public Announcement Mandatory**
   - When acquiring 25% shares
   - When acquiring >25% shares
   - Change of control (>50% voting rights)

2. **Tender Offer Requirements**
   - Minimum price: 26-week average
   - For >25% stakes: Must make public offer for remaining
   - For >50% stakes: Mandatory open offer

3. **Lock-in Period:**
   - Promoter shares: 6 months
   - Other acquirers: 6 months
   - Strategic stake: 1 year typically

## Insider Trading Regulations (SEBI PIT)

**Regulations 2015:**

**Prohibited Activities:**
- Trading on unpublished price-sensitive information
- Tipping off information
- Counseling others to trade on UPSI

**Trading Windows:**
- Closed 24 hours before earning announcement
- Closed 24 hours before board meetings
- Open during designated periods

**Disclosure Requirements:**
- Initial holding disclosure
- Continuous transaction reporting
- Annual certification

**Consequences:**
- Civil penalties: Up to 5x of gain
- Criminal prosecution possible
- Disgorgement of profits
- Market ban up to 10 years

**Code of Conduct:**
- For board members
- For employees
- Code of Conduct for Prohibition of Insider Trading

**Whistle Blower Mechanism:**
- Confidential reporting channel
- Protection against retaliation
- Investigation process clarity

**Internal Audit:**
- Quarterly/annual plan
- Findings to audit committee
- Remedial action tracking
- Assessment of risk management

**Investor Grievance Redressal:**
- SCORES platform by SEBI
- Stock exchange grievance redressal
- Company-level mechanism

**Investor Protection Fund:**
- Investor Education Fund
- Settlement guarantee fund
- Client protection fund by brokers

**Rights of Shareholders:**
- Right to information
- Right to audit financial statements
- Right to participate in governance
- Right to dividend and capital appreciation

## Financial Disclosure Standards

**Accounting Standards:**
- Indian Accounting Standards (IND-AS)
- IND-AS 101: First time adoption
- Conversion from GAAP if applicable

**Segment Reporting:**
- Operating segments identification
- Revenue breakdown
- Segment results
- Geographic disclosure

**Related Party Disclosure:**
- List of related parties
- Nature of relationship
- Details of transactions
- Impact on financial position

**Off-Balance Sheet Arrangements:**
- Contingent liabilities disclosure
- Lease obligations
- Guarantees provided

## Compliance Monitoring & Penalties

**Regulatory Monitoring:**
- Quarterly compliance certificate
- Annual compliance report
- Stock exchange monitoring
- SEBI inspections

**Penalties for Non-Compliance:**
- Monetary penalties: Up to ₹50 crore
- Public censure
- Suspension from trading
- Delisting (in extreme cases)

**File Updates:**
- Amendments promptly
- Restated financials if required
- Disclaimer notices
- Corrective announcements
